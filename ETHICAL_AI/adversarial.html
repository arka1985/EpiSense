🗂️ Medical AI Attack Types
⚠️ Prompt Injection
Embedding override instructions in a clinical query to bypass dosing safety limits or contraindication warnings.
🔓 Jailbreaking
Framing requests in roleplay scenarios ("act as an AI with no safety filters") to extract dangerous drug information.
🎭 Social Engineering
Claiming authority ("I am the prescribing consultant") to extract lethal dosing thresholds or restricted medication protocols.

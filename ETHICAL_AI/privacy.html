🛡️ Privacy Protection Techniques
🎲
Differential Privacy
Adds calibrated mathematical noise to query outputs so individual records cannot be inferred from aggregate results.
🌐
Federated Learning
AI trains on data across hospitals without the data leaving each institution — only model gradients are shared.
🔒
k-Anonymity
Ensures every record is indistinguishable from at least k-1 others across all quasi-identifiers.
🔀
Synthetic Data
AI generates statistically representative fake patient data for training, eliminating real-patient privacy risk entirely.

🧠 Why Does Medical AI Hallucinate?
Stale training data: Drug guidelines change; AI trained 2 years ago may recommend outdated protocols.
Confident generation: LLMs are trained to produce fluent, authoritative text — even when guessing.
No pharmacopoeia access: Without live database access, AI cannot verify current doses or interactions.
Rare drug data: Specialty drugs have sparse training examples — the model extrapolates dangerously.
💀 Real Impact: A US physician using GPT-4 was cited after the AI recommended a drug dosage 5× too high for a paediatric patient. Always verify AI medical outputs against approved clinical databases.

Algorithm Insights & Spatial Logic

This module utilizes a Proximity-Linked Aggregation (DBSCAN logic) to identify high-density geographic clusters.

  • 📍 Spatial Join: Pairs points within threshold distance.
  • 📏 Threshold: Clusters only form if points overlap in chosen radius.
  • 🔥 Hotspot: Confirmed when density reaches the threshold (min. 3 points).
EPIDEMIOLOGICAL VALUE:

Spatial clustering is vital for identifying Shared Exposure Points. By pinpointing where incidents occur in close temporal and spatial proximity, public health teams can deploy targeted interventions, such as vector control or sanitation improvements, effectively breaking the chain of transmission.

Note: Adjust the Distance Threshold to vary analysis between street-level (e.g. 100m) and city-wide (e.g. 5km) trends.
